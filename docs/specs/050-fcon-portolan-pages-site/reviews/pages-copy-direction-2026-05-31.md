# Pages Copy And Visual Direction

**Date**: 2026-05-31

## Decision

Use a restrained static product page:

- first screen names FCON and Portolan immediately;
- copy says Portolan prepares local evidence for agents;
- primary routes point to install, demo, product claims, GitHub, contributing, security, and support;
- visuals are product-relevant text panels and artifact summaries, not screenshots of unverified UI or decorative hype.

## Rejected Alternatives

- Generic AI consulting hero: too broad and likely to outrun product evidence.
- UI screenshots: current evidence is CLI/artifact based, not a polished UI product.
- Generated decorative imagery: unnecessary and less inspectable than product artifacts.

## Why Now

First-screen direction was a blocker for T006. The v1 page needs enough polish to be credible without implying unverified adoption, UI behavior, or broad benchmark proof.

## Reversibility

Medium. Public copy can change, but initial launch text may be quoted.

## Risk If Wrong

The page may feel quieter than a marketing site. That is acceptable for v1 because claim accuracy is more important than conversion polish.

## Confidence

high
