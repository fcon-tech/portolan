# Conduct Policy Decision

**Date**: 2026-05-30

## Decision

Use a lightweight `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md` based on Contributor Covenant principles,
scoped for a small maintainer-led open-source project.

## Rejected Alternatives

- Copy the full Contributor Covenant text without tailoring.
- Publish no conduct guidance.
- Invent a private conduct mailbox that has not been created or monitored.

## Why Now

Public GitHub contributors need a visible behavior boundary before community
files and issue templates invite contribution.

## Reversibility

High. The policy can be replaced with the full Contributor Covenant or a
dedicated private reporting route later.

## Risk If Wrong

Sensitive conduct reports may still lack a private non-security channel. The
policy therefore tells reporters not to publish sensitive personal details
publicly until a dedicated contact exists.

## Confidence

medium
